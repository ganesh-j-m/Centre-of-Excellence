Our Work & Goals

Making Village Colleges Better Places to Learn

Study Space for Local Students

We set up good study rooms and reading halls in villages so students can prepare for government exams like UPSC and MPSC without leaving their hometown.

Online Classes by Expert Teachers

Students in villages can now get the same quality of teaching as city students — through live online classes by expert teachers for UPSC and MPSC exams.

Help to Start Your Own Business

We encourage students to become job creators, not just job seekers — by giving money and support to students who want to start their own small business.

Results

What Change Will This Bring?

When colleges get better tools and support, students grow — and so do their communities.

No Need to Leave Home

Students can stay with their families in their village and still get top-quality study materials and training for government jobs and private companies.

Money Used the Right Way

College principals and local coordinators keep a close eye to make sure every rupee is used properly and reaches the students who need it.

Student Startups Get Support

Students with new business ideas get seed money and expert guidance to turn their ideas into real, working businesses.
