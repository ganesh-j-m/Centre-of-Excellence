# 🏢 Centre of Excellence - Higher & Technical Education Dept. & MITSC

An official, fully responsive web application designed for the **Centre of Excellence** initiative—a joint program by the **Higher & Technical Education Department, Government of Maharashtra** and **MITSC**. This platform is dedicated to bridging the educational gap for rural students, empowering them with city-like facilities directly in their hometowns.

🚀 **Live Project Link:** [https://ganesh-j-m.github.io/Centre-of-Excellence/](https://ganesh-j-m.github.io/Centre-of-Excellence/)

---

## 🌟 Core Features & Goals

- **🏫 Study Space for Local Students:** Setting up well-equipped study rooms and reading halls in village colleges so students can prepare for competitive exams like UPSC and MPSC without relocating.
- **💻 Expert Online Classes:** Delivering high-quality live and recorded lectures by expert educators to ensure equal learning opportunities for rural and urban candidates alike.
- **💼 Entrepreneurship Support:** Providing structured guidance, resources, and mentorship to help local students kickstart their own business ventures.
- **🎨 Government & Institutional Branding:** Features a clean, highly secure, and professional layout embedded with official Maharashtra State Government and MITSC symbols.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack Used

- **Frontend Architecture:** HTML5, Modern CSS3 for sleek layouts.
- **Frameworks & Grid System:** Bootstrap 5 (Ensuring 100% responsiveness across desktops, tablets, and smartphones).
- **Typography:** Professional web-safe fonts optimized for governmental portals.

---

## 📸 Interface Preview

The user interface follows an intuitive structural grid optimized for easy navigation:

- **Top Navigation:** Quick links for *Home, What We Do, Funding Details,* and *Our Impact*.
- **Hero Section:** Prominent institutional banner showcasing corporate alignment between the Maharashtra Government and MITSC.
- **Impact Showcase:** Dynamic content layouts highlighting recent success milestones, educational award distribution, and institutional growth photos.
